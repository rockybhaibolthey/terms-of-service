Payments can be made in cash at the time of pickup or through third-party payment options (e.g., UPI, mobile wallets).

Booking a Pickup

When you book a scrap pickup through the Apashamkirikiri platform, you will be required to:

  • Select the pickup type (e.g., Mass, Supermarket, General, ASAP)
  • Enter the estimated weight of the scrap

Once you confirm your pickup details, Apashamkirikiri will arrive on the scheduled date to collect your scrap. The price for each type of recyclable material is displayed in the Apashamkirikiri app at the time of booking. The prices are calculated per kilogram and will vary depending on the material. Prices are locked at the time of booking and will not change between booking and pickup.

Types of Recyclable Materials

Examples of recyclable materials accepted by Apashamkirikiri include:

  • Paper (e.g., cardboard, newspaper)
  • Glass (e.g., bottles, jars)
  • Electronics (e.g., old phones, wires, electronic scrap)

Prices are transparent and based on current market conditions. Apashamkirikiri strives to provide accurate pricing but prices are subject to change based on market fluctuations.

The final payment will be calculated by multiplying the total weight of your recyclables by the locked price per kilogram.

No Adjustments Post-Booking

Once the price is locked at booking, no adjustments to the price will be made, except in cases of significant weight discrepancies.
